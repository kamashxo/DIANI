The Eiffel Tower is a wrought-iron lattice tower located on the Champ de Mars in Paris, France. It was designed and built by engineer Gustave Eiffel and his company between 1887 and 1889. The eiffel tower is the most visited place in the whole of paris, it having about 2million visitors a year.
